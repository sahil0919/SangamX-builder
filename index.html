Why Societal Markets?

1

We believe that bringing rigorous thinking to the use of money and resources in (to) the world of social enterprises, will significantly multiply the impact being delivered.

2

We believe that incorporating the good of free-market mechanisms and creating a similar ecosystem appropriate for NPOs and Social Enterprises will multiply the delivered impact. We are calling this the Societal Markets.

3

We believe that instilling a sense of competitiveness through a free-market mechanism continuously increase effectiveness of social impact organisations, thereby continuously increasing the social impact from resources.

4

We also believe that any re-design of these current industry structures needs to moderate adverse effects of capitalism from the get-go.
