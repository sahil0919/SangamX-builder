Billions are wasted on ineffective
philanthropy.

Philanthropy is decades behind business in applying rigorous thinking to the use of money.


Dr. Michael Porter, Harvard Business School

Despite annual global contributions of $2.5+ T to social enterprises, and having nearly 10% of the private sector workforce deployed in the non-profit sector, we have not materially moved the needle on societal impact.

  • Income gap is largest in 50 years
  • About 10 per cent of the world population, still live in extreme poverty today
  • Almost 75 per cent of the earth’s surface has been altered, threatening 1 million animal species into extinction
  • Not enough progress on other critical social needs either – climate, education, sanitation, water etc.
