Impact Ticker

The Impact Ticker provides a near real-time, continuous, easy-to-consume valuation of the organization's ongoing ability to deliver on its goals. This is in stark contrast to what is available today - static, self-disclosure based “star” ratings that have not been able to establish credibility and trust at scale so far.

On the other hand, the Impact Ticker
  • Provides a direct correlation between investment and impact
  • Drives organisations to focus on their credibility and implement trusted practices and behaviours
  • Delivers continuous, visible scrutiny establishes credibility for the investors and reduces cost of finding good investment ideas
  • Deepens trust between NPOs and Investors, that expand the per-capita investment as well as increasing the number of impact investors

Why is it pivotal to the success of Societal Markets?

Establish relative performance of an organization with its peers
  • The impact ticker assessment model factors relative performance of organisations
  • This embeds a DNA of competitiveness amongst the organisations. This DNA continuously drives them to use their resources effectively and be better than their peers - a behavior that is the CSF of free market mechanism
  • Drives continuous improvements, and innovation

Currently : No relative benchmarks are available

Capture the ultimate verdict of effectiveness – verdict of consumer
  • Poorly delivered products and services will turn customers away resulting in revenue loss, impacting their stock price.
  • Similarly, the Impact Ticker – through its “Point of Impact” infrastructure will correlate verdict of the beneficiary (consumer) into its Impact Ticker. This in turn drives the organization to intensely focus on the impact delivered to the beneficiary.

Currently: No such dynamic mechanism is available

Aligns employee behaviours to long term success of the org
  • Compensation of the employees are linked to the LT sustained performance of the organization – to its stock price/ to its Impact Ticker
  • Equips the organization with a key tool to attract the best talent who can deliver best-in-class solutions

Currently : not possible to design such compensation tools

Key bridge to transition from IPO to Secondary Markets
  • Demand in the secondary markets establishes organizations' ongoing ability to deliver on its objectives and not just marketing led demand at listing
  • Impact Ticker precisely articulates the ability of NPOs/SEs to sustain and grow impact
  • It provides the basis on which investors can flexibly enter/ exit their holdings

Currently : no such mechanism exists

Impact Ticker: Key capabilities to be created by SangamX

  • Valuation: Pioneer the assessment models of NPOs & SEs so that the Impact Ticker that evaluates the instruments traded on the SSE, is a direct and clear indicator of the NPO’s ongoing effectiveness in delivering on its stated social purpose
  • The valuation model will draw on the information available through disclosures, and combine that with our two unique industry leading inputs:
    • 1. “Specialists”
    • a. Highly credible, long standing NPOs who provide an independent on-the ground assessment
    • b. They are specialists in either – a particular UN SDGs or in a geographical area
    • c. Will provide an additional, independent narrative on the claims of the NPO and research firms
    • d. Paid for this service only by SangamX, to ensure independence and neutrality
    • 2. Digital “Point-of-Impact” infrastructure to capture beneficiary input at the point of impact delivery
    • a. Proxy for customer buying behaviour
    • b. Input of the beneficiaries will influence the valuation directly
    • These two unique inputs will be combined with other currently available research ratings, disclosures, social audit findings etc into an aggregated assessment model will create a continuous, live “impact ticker”.
